Everything you need to ship a landing page with Awesome Crew.

Introduction

Awesome Crew turns a single prompt into a fully-editable landing page. Describe your business, and our models produce the hero, feature blocks, pricing, and call-to-action — styled, responsive, and ready to deploy.

Quickstart

  1. Create an account from the sign in page.
  2. From your dashboard, click New page.
  3. Describe your business in one sentence.
  4. Review and tweak the generated page.
  5. Click Publish to make it live.

Authentication

We support email + password, Google, and GitHub. All sessions are stored securely via Supabase Auth with row-level security enforced on every request.

Generating a page

Each generation uses your monthly quota. Free accounts get 3 generations per month, Starter gets 20, and Pro is unlimited.

Editing content

After generation, every section is editable in place — headlines, paragraphs, features, pricing tiers, and CTA links.

Publishing

Publishing a page makes it accessible at your Awesome Crew subdomain. You can unpublish at any time from the dashboard.

Custom domains

Pro plan includes custom domain support with automatic SSL. Point a CNAME record to pages.awesomecrew.app.

Plans & limits

  • Free — 3 pages/month, Awesome Crew subdomain
  • Starter — 20 pages/month, custom meta tags
  • Pro — Unlimited pages, custom domains, priority support

FAQ

Can I export the HTML?

Yes — paid plans include one-click static HTML export.

Do you collect analytics on my pages?

We collect aggregate traffic data for uptime purposes only. We never track your visitors.
