Awesome Crew v2.0

Improvement March 28, 2026

Supabase row-level security hardening

Tightened RLS policies on the pages table so users can only read, update, or delete their own rows. Public reads are limited to rows where published = true.

Fix March 20, 2026

Fixed localhost redirect in magic links

OAuth and magic-link flows now always return to the production domain, regardless of where the browser was pointed at the time of sign-in.

Feature March 10, 2026

Publish / Unpublish toggle

Toggle a page live or back to draft from the dashboard in one click — useful for quick experiments and seasonal pages.

v1.0 Launch February 1, 2026

Initial release

First public release. Generate landing pages from a text prompt. Google sign-in, a simple dashboard, and a template gallery.
