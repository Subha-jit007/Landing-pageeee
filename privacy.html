Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

Awesome Crew ("we", "us", "our") builds tools that let you generate landing pages from a single prompt. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, and (if you sign in with Google or GitHub) your name and avatar.
  • Content: the prompts you submit and the landing pages you generate, edit and publish.
  • Usage data: pages generated, session timestamps, feature interactions — used to enforce plan limits and improve the product.
  • Device data: IP address, browser type and operating system, collected automatically when you use the site.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain and improve the service.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To enforce plan limits and process payments.
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account notifications).
  • To detect, prevent and respond to abuse or security issues.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Infrastructure providers (Supabase for database and auth, Vercel for hosting).
  • AI model providers to generate your pages from your prompts.
  • Law enforcement when required by valid legal process.

5. Cookies and local storage

We use strictly necessary cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, which removes your profile and all associated pages within 30 days.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export or delete your personal data. Email privacy@awesomecrew.app to exercise these rights.

8. Security

Data is encrypted in transit with TLS and at rest. Access to production systems is restricted and audited. We follow Supabase row-level security so users can only access their own rows.

9. Children

Awesome Crew is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via email or an in-product notice.

11. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@awesomecrew.app.
